Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 17, 2026

Overview

Cem Verbos is built with your privacy in mind. The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All your learning progress stays on your device and, if you choose to enable it, in your own iCloud account.

Data Collection

Cem Verbos does not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No third-party SDKs or services
  • No advertising or ad networks
  • No cookies or web tracking
  • No account creation required

Data Storage

Your learning progress, settings, and history are stored locally on your device. No data is sent to any external server.

iCloud Sync

If iCloud is enabled on your device, Cem Verbos can sync your progress across your Apple devices using Apple's iCloud Key-Value storage. This data is stored in your personal iCloud account and is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. The developer does not have access to your iCloud data.

In-App Purchases

Cem Verbos offers an optional one-time purchase handled entirely by Apple's StoreKit. All payment processing is managed by Apple. The developer does not collect, store, or have access to any payment or financial information.

Children's Privacy

Cem Verbos does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Since the app does not collect data, significant changes are unlikely.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us at cizeon@cizeon.xyz.
